Associate at Wipro









Role Purpose

The role aims to deliver efficient technical support for the process, actively resolving client issues either directly or through timely escalation to meet process SLAs.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Support the process by managing transactions in accordance with required quality standards.
  2. Handle all incoming help requests from clients via telephone and/or emails courteously.
  3. Document essential end-user identification information, including name, department, contact information, and the nature of the problem.
  4. Update availability in the RAVE system for enhanced process productivity.
  5. Record, track, and document all queries, including problem-solving steps and resolutions.
  6. Follow standard processes and procedures to address client queries within defined SLAs.
  7. Access and maintain internal knowledge bases, resources, and FAQs for effective problem resolution.
  8. Learn and understand product details to enhance client interaction and troubleshooting.
  9. Document and analyze call logs to identify trends and prevent future problems.
  10. Maintain and update self-help documents for customers to expedite resolution.
  11. Identify red flags and escalate serious client issues to the Team Leader for untimely resolution.
  12. Ensure compliance with service agreements to avoid legal challenges.

Delivering Excellent Customer Service

  1. Diagnose and troubleshoot client queries effectively.
  2. Provide product support and resolution by guiding users through step-by-step solutions.
  3. Assist clients in navigating product menus and understanding features.
  4. Troubleshoot all client queries in a user-friendly, courteous, and professional manner.
  5. Maintain logs and records of all customer queries as per standard procedures.
  6. Process and record all incoming calls and emails accurately using designated tracking software.
  7. Offer alternative solutions to clients where appropriate to retain their business.
  8. Organize ideas and communicate oral messages effectively.

Build Capability for Operational Excellence

  1. Undertake product trainings to stay current with features, changes, and updates.
  2. Enroll in product-specific and other trainings per client requirements.
  3. Collaborate with team leaders to identify training themes and learning issues.
  4. Update job knowledge through self-learning opportunities and maintaining personal networks.
