Customer Service Quality Executive (Remote) at Ditto









About Ditto

Ditto is on a mission to empower users by providing a comprehensive understanding of insurance, guiding them through choices, avoiding common pitfalls, and facilitating well-informed decisions. The company offers expert insurance advice, smooth policy purchases, and dedicated claim support to ensure a seamless end-to-end insurance experience.

Ditto, as part of the Finshots family and backed by Zerodha, has pioneered the industry-first "No-spam" policy, demonstrating a commitment to user-centric solutions. With over three years of experience, Ditto has assisted more than 300,000 users with personalized insurance advice.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Monitor calls and provide actionable insights to enhance current processes.
  • Contribute to the development, creation, and implementation of quality processes.
  • Gather information to improve advisor performance.
  • Provide feedback on the quality of advisor recommendations.
  • Develop customer support quality standards.

Experience and Skills

  • 0-3 years of experience.
  • Strong communication and customer service skills.
  • Sense of ownership and leadership qualities.
  • Excellent listening and analytical skills.
  • Effective communication and collaboration abilities.

Application Process

If your application is shortlisted, you will receive an email with a link to record a Short Video Resume. It is mandatory to complete this process.

Perks

  • Remote work opportunities.
  • Comprehensive health insurance coverage for you and your family.
  • CTC - 4,51,600 LPA.
