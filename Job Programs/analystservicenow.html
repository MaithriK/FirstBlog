ServiceNow Business Analyst

The ServiceNow Business Analyst at KPMG plays a key role in designing, developing, and implementing IT, GRC, Cyber, and Risk processes. This position involves research, analysis, and the configuration or administration of ServiceNow Platform modules. The ideal candidate should have 0 to 2 years of experience in IRM, GRC, Cyber SecOps, with expertise in business analysis, Scrum Methodology, and SDLC processes.

Key Qualifications:

  • 0 to 2 years of experience in IRM, GRC, Cyber SecOps processes
  • Proficiency in business analysis and ServiceNow Platform modules
  • Understanding of Scrum Methodology
  • Familiarity with SDLC processes and system design
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Previous experience with GRC tools/platforms like Archer, MetricStream, Open Pages
  • Interpersonal and analytical skills for effective collaboration and conflict resolution
  • Experience in communicating across all organizational levels

Responsibilities:

The ServiceNow Business Analyst will contribute to ServiceNow IRM, SecOps Transformation Projects, involving:

  1. Gathering requirements from business users and translating them into specifications.
  2. Documenting workflows and obtaining user sign-off on specifications.
  3. Collaborating with the technical team to translate functionality into application architecture.
  4. Communicating business directives and goals to the technical team.
  5. Designing and executing test plans to ensure proper implementation of requirements.
  6. Understanding long-term business objectives and suggesting innovative strategies.
  7. Assisting in content development, recommending enhancements, and identifying new requirements.
  8. Applying knowledge of system capacity and recommending upgrades.
  9. Collaborating with IT application analysts and programmers as necessary.
  10. Conducting analysis of department production and redesigning workflows if necessary.
  11. Developing, editing, and maintaining operating procedures and standards manuals.
  12. Serving as a consultant to improve internal procedures for other organizations.
  13. Conducting training sessions to implement new systems and procedures.
  14. Performing at or above enterprise performance standards.
  15. Assuming additional responsibilities as assigned.

Qualifications:

Preferred qualifications include BE/BTech, ME/MTech graduates. Certifications preferred but not mandatory: ServiceNow Certified System Administrator (must have), Business Analyst certification (IIBA, PMI - PBA, or equivalent), CISA, CISM, ISO 27001.
