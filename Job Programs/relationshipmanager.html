PROGRAM OVERVIEW

In collaboration with ICICI Bank, NIIT IFBI has curated a comprehensive training program and formulated pertinent course content to refine the soft and hard skills essential for contemporary banking, resulting in professionals ready for the industry.

The program is executed through NIIT's digital platform, where adept faculty members involve participants in diverse experiential activities. Participants engage with faculty and peers, partake in video-based role-plays, and submit assignments following the curriculum plan.

  • Comprehensive understanding of ICICI Bank's products, processes, and policies
  • Holistic insight into the Banking industry
  • Acquisition of skills and techniques for promoting banking and financial products

ELIGIBILITY

Educational Qualification

Achieved >= 50% aggregate in X, XII, and Graduation.

Age Criteria

The applicant should be between 19-25 years of age at the time of program admission.

Additional Criteria

  • Possession of PAN card and Aadhaar card is mandatory, with copies to be submitted during the selection process.
  • Candidates with less than 1 year of work experience are eligible to apply.
