Axis Bank Young Bankers Program


Manipal axis bank young bankers program

The Axis Bank Young Bankers program provides young graduates with a head-start towards a career in banking. The program identifies young bankers and equips them with necessary banking knowledge and skills through Learn-Earn-Grow model. The curriculum is a mix of classroom sessions, role playing and internships which helps students develop a comprehensive understanding of banking. On successful completion of the program, students are awarded a Post Graduate Diploma in Banking Services along with an opportunity to join Axis Bank. We offer a one year, full-time residential program with campus in Manipal Global Education, Bangalore. Come, take charge of your progress!

NIIT axis bank young bankers program

Axis Bank has partnered with NIIT for Young Bankers Program to create New Age Bankers. The selection process and program structure are co-created with Axis Bank to provide Knowledge, Skills and Earning opportunities within the program. NIIT's ABYB is a One Year Post Graduate Diploma Program offered at NIIT University in Neemrana, Rajasthan as a full-time residential program. NIIT creates possibilities for highly-motivated aspirants who are desirous of specific career opportunities, hence are willing to invest in making themselves ready for selected career.
