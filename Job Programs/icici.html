ICICI Bank Manipal Probationary Officer's Programme

Program Overview

The ICICI Bank Manipal Probationary Officer's Programme represents a collaborative effort between ICICI Bank and Manipal Global Education Services, aiming to provide a pathway for ambitious graduates to establish a rewarding career in the banking sector. Over the past 15 years, more than 30,000 candidates have successfully joined ICICI Bank through this initiative.

Upon successful completion of the programme, candidates are guaranteed employment with ICICI Bank.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Education: Candidates should possess a graduation degree or engineering qualification from any discipline.
  • Qualifying Marks: A minimum of 55% aggregate marks is required.
  • Age: Applicants should be below 27 years of age.

Remuneration

  • Stipend during the programme: Participants will receive a total stipend ranging from ₹2.32 lakh to ₹2.60 lakh throughout the duration of the programme.
  • Starting Salary (CTC): The starting salary is in the range of ₹5.00 lakh to ₹5.50 lakh, location-dependent.

Selection Process

The selection process for the ICICI Bank Probationary Officer programme includes:

  1. Step 1: Online Aptitude Test
  2. Step 2: Online Personal Interview

Program Highlights

Term I (4 months): Initiates with classroom training at ICICI Manipal Academy (IMA) in Bengaluru, renowned as one of India's premier educational institutes. Participants receive a monthly stipend of ₹5,000.

Term II (2 months): Involves an internship at ICICI Bank, providing hands-on experience in a professional setting. Interns receive a monthly stipend ranging from ₹22,000 to ₹24,000.

Term III (6 months): Encompasses on-the-job training at ICICI Bank, empowering participants to develop professionally. A monthly stipend of ₹28,000 to ₹32,000 is provided.
