Speech Generation Intern at Sony









Sony Corporation, headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, stands as a multinational conglomerate founded on May 7, 1946, by Masaru Ibuka and Akio Morita. Over the years, Sony has evolved into one of the world's largest and most renowned electronics and entertainment companies. Operating across various sectors, including consumer and professional electronics, gaming, entertainment, and financial services, Sony has left an indelible mark on the global business landscape.

Business Divisions

In the realm of electronics, Sony is a prolific producer of consumer goods, manufacturing an extensive range of products such as televisions, audio equipment, cameras, and smartphones. Sony's influence in the gaming industry is prominent through its PlayStation brand, managed by Sony Interactive Entertainment. The PlayStation consoles, games, and associated services significantly contribute to Sony's revenue. Sony Pictures Entertainment and Sony Music Entertainment are integral components of the entertainment division, engaged in film and television production and global music distribution, respectively.

PlayStation

Sony's presence in the gaming industry has been marked by the success of the PlayStation brand, which became a dominant force since the launch of the original PlayStation console in 1994. Subsequent iterations, including the PS2, PS3, PS4, and PS5, have achieved immense popularity worldwide.

Sony Pictures

Within the entertainment sector, Sony Pictures Entertainment is responsible for film and television production and distribution, with notable franchises like Spider-Man and Men in Black. Simultaneously, Sony Music Entertainment holds a significant position among the "Big Three" global record companies, representing numerous artists across various genres and owning multiple record labels.

Consumer Electronics

Renowned for its consumer electronics, Sony's product lineup includes Bravia TVs, Xperia smartphones, Alpha cameras, and a diverse range of audio products.

Innovation and Technology

Sony has a rich history of technological innovation, playing a pivotal role in the development of iconic technologies such as the Walkman, Compact Disc (CD), and the Blu-ray disc.

Financial Services

Beyond electronics and entertainment, Sony operates in financial services, providing insurance and banking services. Sony Financial Holdings, Inc. oversees these financial activities.

Corporate Culture

The corporate culture of Sony is characterized by a commitment to quality and innovation. The company's slogan, "Be Moved," reflects its dedication to creating products and experiences that emotionally resonate with consumers.

Global Presence

With a global presence in numerous countries and regions, Sony ensures accessibility to its products and services on a worldwide scale.

Challenges and Resilience

In navigating the challenges of the ever-evolving tech industry, Sony has demonstrated resilience by adapting to market changes and consistently delivering products that captivate consumers.

In conclusion, Sony Corporation's indelible mark on technology and entertainment is evident across diverse industries. The brand is synonymous with innovation, quality, and a commitment to creating transformative experiences for consumers globally.

Sony Research India

About the Job

Sony Research India, a global hub for cutting-edge research and development, has positioned itself at the forefront of technological innovation. With laboratories strategically located in Japan, the United States, Europe, and Asia, the organization endeavors to push the boundaries of technology, contributing to the diverse businesses within the Sony Group. From electronics and entertainment to financial fields, Sony Research India plays a pivotal role in sustaining Sony's legacy of excellence.

The significance of Sony Research India's role in the creation of new technologies, products, and services cannot be overstated. The organization is not just a research center; it is a driving force shaping the future of the industry. By staying committed to advancing technology, Sony Research India actively contributes to the dynamic landscape of global innovation. This commitment is particularly evident in the organization's emphasis on AI and data analytics, two domains that are increasingly recognized as transformative in the digital era.

Key responsibility

  • The current career opportunities at Sony Research India (SRI) reflect the organization's forward-looking approach.
  • Prospective candidates are invited to contribute to daily basic development activities, collaborating closely with research scientists and team members.
  • The focus on speech generation-related research topics adds a layer of sophistication to the roles, with an explicit goal of developing High-Quality Controllable Text-to-Speech and Emotional Voice Cloning Systems for Indian languages.
  • This not only reflects the organization's commitment to technological advancement but also its dedication to linguistic and cultural inclusivity.

Work Location and Duration

The flexibility offered in work locations further enhances the attractiveness of these opportunities. Candidates can opt for remote work or choose to be based in vibrant technological hubs like Bangalore or Mumbai. The anticipated 6-month engagement, starting in the first week of March 2024, requires full-time commitment from 9:00 to 18:00, Monday to Friday, providing a structured yet dynamic work environment.

Essential Education / Qualifications / Experience

  • Educational qualifications and experiences required for these roles reflect the organization's commitment to excellence.
  • A Ph.D. or Master's degree in Computer Science or a Bachelor's degree with hands-on experience in Generative models with applications in audio/speech are foundational prerequisites.

Must have skills

  • Proficiency in Pytorch
  • Prior experience in developing text-to-speech solutions for Indian languages
  • Deep understanding of state-of-the-art Text-to-Speech and Voice Cloning technologies
  • Additionally, a robust grasp of AI generative models is essential for successful candidates

Those possessing additional skills, such as prior experience or understanding of Generative AI-based concepts like Diffusion Models/LLM, find themselves at a distinctive advantage in the selection process.
