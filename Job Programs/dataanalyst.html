TRAINEE - JUNIOR DATA ANALYST at Cognizant









Qualification:

The individual should hold a graduate/post-graduate/doctorate degree in life sciences, pharmacy, or medical sciences, or be a registered nurse.

Responsibility:

Business/Customer:

  • Limited customer interaction under guidance.
  • Understanding of domain processes, sub-processes, functions, and terminologies (such as SOPs, QC checklists).
  • For PV/Complaints Management, individuals in this role perform data entry from source documents into the respective clinical/safety database. During this activity, the associate is responsible for meeting turnaround times and maintaining accuracy.

CODING:

  • Perform coding activities on the assigned project with timelines and efficiency.
  • Import uncoded terms into the database and export coded medical terms from the coding platform.
  • Query Management.
  • Create "New Term Request" and prioritize.
  • Perform Dictionary upversioning activity as and when required.

CDM:

  • Limited to study conduct activities.
  • Data review and query management are done with assistance.
  • Limited email conversations with the client.
  • Every activity requires Peer QC by a supervisor or allocated seniors.
  • No involvement in Set Up and Migration activities.
  • Limited involvement in data pad locking activities during timelines of Database locks.

Project/Process:

  • Handles the first level processing of assigned transactions. Complex problems (procedures/processes) are referred to the supervisor/line manager.
  • Adhere to quality requirements, achieve targets/volumes in the given TAT (Turnaround time).
  • Adhere to mandatory industry regulations and compliance requirements for the given process.

Knowledge Management:

  • Understand various document templates and standards (such as SOPs) to interpret the documents & related terminologies.

People/Team Management:

  • Adhere to organizational hygiene and compliance needs in terms of timesheet submission, assimilation & attendance.

Good To Have Skills

  • Pharmacovigilance & Safety Ops
  • PV Case Processing

Employee Status: Full-Time Employee

Shift: Day Job

Travel: No

Job Posting: Aug 10, 2021
