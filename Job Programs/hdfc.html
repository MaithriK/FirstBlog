Manipal Global Academy of BFSI

In collaboration with the Manipal Global Academy of BFSI, HDFC Bank has launched a unique recruitment initiative, Future Bankers 2.0. The program aims to groom recent graduates into proficient banking professionals within one year. This comprehensive initiative features a blend of classroom sessions, guest lectures, group discussions, role plays, and fieldwork.

The curriculum also includes an eight-month paid internship and on-the-job training at HDFC Bank branches, providing hands-on experience in banking products, compliance, and day-to-day operations.

Upon successful completion, participants will receive a Postgraduate Diploma in Sales & Relationship Banking from the Manipal Academy of BFSI, securing an assured job opportunity as a personal banker at the Deputy Manager level within HDFC Bank. Successful candidates will be offered an annual CTC of up to Rs. 5.59 lakh.

Vinay Razdan, Chief Human Resources Officer at HDFC Bank, emphasizes the program's significance, offering postgraduates the chance to launch their careers in a certified Great Place to Work. He highlights the competitive pay scale and the opportunity to 'earn while they learn.' The initiative aims to mentor new hires into responsible bankers, rewarding competence and performance while ensuring equal opportunities for all.

Robin Bhowmik, Chief Business Officer at Manipal Global, expresses excitement about the long-term partnership, focusing on creating a skilled candidate pool proficient in both banking and customer-facing roles. The program aims to equip postgraduates with in-depth banking knowledge through on-the-job training, ensuring consistency in service quality standards across HDFC Bank branches.

Candidates interested in Future Bankers 2.0 can apply through the HDFC Bank Future Bankers Website. HDFC Bank, with an active employee base of over 1,70,000, is committed to shaping young futures through this initiative.

For more information about Manipal Global Academy of BFSI, visit www.manipalgroup.com. To learn more about HDFC Bank, please visit www.hdfcbank.com.
