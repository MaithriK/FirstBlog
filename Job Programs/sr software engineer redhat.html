Senior Software Engineer - Data management at Redhat









About the job:

Join our highly focused Data Development, Insights & Strategy (DDIS) team at Red Hat, where we lead digital-first execution and transformation by strategically leveraging data for our customers, partners, and associates. We are seeking talented engineers to lead the technology and design of significant parts of Red Hat's Data Development Platform. As part of our team, you'll contribute to the creation of next-generation product and customer 360 applications.

We are seeking talented engineers to lead the technology and design of significant parts of Red Hat's Data Development Platform. As part of our team, you'll contribute to the creation of next-generation product and customer 360 applications. We value innovation, creativity, excellent communication, and collaboration skills. If you thrive in a startup-like environment with a keen attention to detail and a passion for quality and open-source software, we want you on our team!

Your Role:

As the voice of Red Hat's technical authority, you will create and deliver features/capabilities around our Product and Customer 360 applications. These applications are built on our master data management platform, facilitating delightful customer experiences for Red Hat's business teams. Collaborate with members of DDIS, finance, operations, sales, marketing, and product during the development process.

What You Will Do:

  • Execute the full software development life cycle through cross-functional work and delivery.
  • Effectively communicate with stakeholders and project team members to ensure visibility into development efforts.
  • Scope incoming requests, design solutions, and recommend new technologies and approaches.
  • Design user scenarios, test cases, and write automated tests.
  • Establish and maintain continuous integration (CI) and continuous delivery (CD) testing environments.
  • Innovate and create Proof of Concepts (PoCs) to solve meaningful business challenges.
  • Define, review, and execute technical best practices.

What You Will Bring:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field.
  • 6+ years of software development experience with a focus on data systems.
  • Experience developing data solutions on cloud infrastructure iteratively.
  • Proficiency in one of the programming languages Go, Python, Java (Java desired).
  • Full software development lifecycle experience, including test automation along with CI/CD.
  • Experience using SQL and normalized data systems.
  • Working experience with MDM solutions like Informatica, Data Warehouses like Snowflake/RedShift, ETL tools like Fivetran, etc., is a huge plus.
  • Experience/knowledge of containers/kubernetes will be advantageous.

Personal Qualities and Communication:

  • Strong communication skills and experience interacting with cross-functional business and engineering teams.
  • Capability in undertaking business needs analysis in direct consultation.
  • Motivated with a passion for quality, learning, and contributing to collective goals.
  • Excellent communication, presentation, and writing skills.
