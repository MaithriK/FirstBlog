Risk Analyst - Risk Governance at Goldman Sachs

Location: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India

Job Description:

The Risk division at The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. is dedicated to managing credit, market, operational, model, independent liquidity risk, and insurance across the firm. As a leading global investment banking and financial services firm, Goldman Sachs is committed to supporting its diverse client base, including corporations, financial institutions, governments, and individuals. Established in 1869, the firm is headquartered in New York with a global presence.

Risk Business Unit:

The Risk Business unit focuses on identifying, monitoring, evaluating, and managing financial and non-financial risks aligned with the firm's Risk Appetite Statement and strategic plan. The Risk Review Group (RRG), reporting to the GS-Group Chief Risk Officer, consists of Credit Review, Risk Testing Group, and Reg Obligations Review Group.

About Risk Testing Group (RTG):

RTG is a multidisciplinary group of quantitative and financial experts operating globally. With a presence in Bangalore, New York, London, Dallas, and Salt Lake City, RTG provides independent oversight of all financial and non-financial risks, ensuring compliance with regulatory and internal expectations. The group reviews qualitative models, data, processes, controls, and systems covering Credit, Market, Operational, and Liquidity risk types.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop a comprehensive understanding of the firm's risk management framework, models, methodology, techniques, and processes.
  • Independently review key regulatory and internal deliverables and communicate results through formal reports.
  • Effectively challenge stakeholder processes and outcomes, liaising across relevant business, technology, and control functions.
  • Prioritize risks, challenge decisions, assumptions, and tolerances, and drive appropriate risk responses.
  • Build and maintain constructive stakeholder relationships, raise action items, and validate remediation plans independently.
  • Contribute to establishing metrics and tools for assessing and reporting inherent risks, control strength, and residual risk consistently and objectively.

Skills and Relevant Experience:

  • Minimum bachelor's degree required; Master's in a quantitative discipline preferred.
  • Exposure to qualitative and quantitative risk management (credit, market, liquidity, and operational risk).
  • Solid knowledge of programming fundamentals, including software development life cycle concepts.
  • Proficient in navigating data sets, developing metrics, and conducting analysis.
  • Proactive with strong analytical orientation, lateral thinking, organizational, and influencing skills.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Robust interpersonal skills with the ability to build relationships and leverage networks within the organization.
  • Interest in financial markets and risk management, motivated by continuous learning and improvement.

About Goldman Sachs:

Goldman Sachs is a global investment banking, securities, and investment management firm committed to fostering diversity and inclusion. Founded in 1869 and headquartered in New York, the firm offers a range of opportunities for professional and personal growth, including training, development, firmwide networks, benefits, wellness programs, and mindfulness initiatives.
